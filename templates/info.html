🤖 About the CSV Analysis Chatbot

Welcome to the CSV Analysis Chatbot — your smart assistant for exploring and understanding tabular data without writing a single line of code!
📊✨ Whether you're a student, analyst, or just curious about what's inside a dataset, this tool is built for you.

🛠️ What can it do?

  • 🔍 Discover column names in your CSV file
  • ❓ Identify missing values in any column
  • 🧹 Clean your data by replacing missing values with column averages
  • 📈 Calculate stats: mean, min, max, count, and sum
  • 🧠 Understand data types for each column
  • 💾 Download your cleaned dataset anytime

💡 Why use it?

Tired of spreadsheets? This chatbot gives you the power of Python’s data tools with the simplicity of a conversation. Just type questions like:

→ What are the column names?
→ Mean of Age
→ Show missing values
→ Replace missing values

It’s perfect for:

  • 📚 Students learning data science
  • 🧾 Non-tech users working with CSV files
  • 🚀 Quick insights without Excel or programming

With its modern UI and smart backend, the chatbot makes data analysis conversational, quick, and accessible for everyone.
